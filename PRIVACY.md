# Privacy Policy for BU 2FA Helper

**Last Updated:** January 2, 2026

## Introduction

BU 2FA Helper ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles your data.

## Data Collection and Storage

### What Data We Collect
- Your TOTP (Time-based One-Time Password) secret
- Usage statistics (number of times you've generated codes)
- User preferences (theme, sound settings, etc.)

### Where Data is Stored
- **All data is stored locally** on your device using Chrome's local storage API
- **No data is transmitted** to any external servers
- **No cloud storage** is used
- **No analytics or tracking** services are implemented

### How Data is Used
- TOTP secret: Used solely to generate 2FA codes for Binghamton University authentication
- Usage statistics: Displayed in the extension popup for your personal reference only
- User preferences: Used to customize your experience with the extension

## Data Sharing

**We do NOT share any data.** Period.

- No data is sent to third parties
- No data is sent to the extension developer
- No data is sent to Binghamton University
- No data leaves your device

## Data Security

### How We Protect Your Data
- Data is stored using Chrome's secure storage API
- All TOTP generation happens locally in your browser
- No network requests are made by the extension
- Open source code allows for security audits

### Important Security Notes
- Anyone with physical access to your computer can potentially access the extension and your stored TOTP secret
- We recommend using this extension only on devices you personally own and control
- Always maintain a backup 2FA method (like Google Authenticator)

## Your Data Rights

### Access
You can view your stored TOTP secret at any time through the extension's settings page.

### Deletion
You can delete your TOTP secret at any time by:
1. Opening the extension settings
2. Clicking the "Delete Secret" button
3. Confirming the deletion

You can also uninstall the extension, which will remove all stored data.

### Export
Your data is stored locally and you maintain full control. You can manually copy your TOTP secret from the settings page.

## Cookies and Tracking

- **No cookies** are used by this extension
- **No tracking** of any kind is implemented
- **No analytics** services are integrated
- **No user identification** or profiling occurs

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms.

## Children's Privacy

This extension is intended for Binghamton University students and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
